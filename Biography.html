EARLY LIFE AND EDUCATION

Andriy was born in Kiev, beautiful ancient city on picturesque coasts of Dnepr river,
capital of Ukraine, when he pursued undergraduate studies at the Art School.

Following graduation in 1977, he enrolled in the Ukrainian National Technical University of Construction and Architecture (KIBI) in Kiev, for graduate work in architecture.
In 1983, after completing his masters diploma thesis, “Planning and development of Recreation
complex in Crimea”, he started his architecture and interior design practice.

Projects:

- Conceptual design of Senior's recreational center in Cascade Estates Village
- Remodelling of luxury Miami Condo and hi-end New York Residence

- Toronto Downtown Condos and Forest Hill residences, offices and retail space renovation
- Chain of Optical Stores for Zeller’s

- Hilton Recreation Center Renovation in Markham
- Jewellery Stores in Promenade and Don Mills Mall and Cafes in Washington DC
